2 Types of Membership


1. Associate Membership

Below 5 thousand Pesos Share Capital

2. Regular Membership

5 thousand Pesos Share Capital and at least 1000.00 Pesos Savings deposit.

Breakdown for Regular Membership:

  • 5000.00 PHP – Share Capital
  • 1000.00 PHP – (At least) Savings Deposit
  • 450.00 PHP – Insurance for 18 years old up to 64 years old
  • 550.00 PHP – Insurance for 65 years old to 75 years old
  • 300.00 PHP – Membership Fee
  • Total: 6750 Pesos – 18 to 64 Years Old
  • 6850 Pesos – 65 to 75 Years Old
